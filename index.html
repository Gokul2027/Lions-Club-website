Our Core Causes

We focus on five key areas of service, aligning with the global mission of Lions Club International to address the pressing needs of our community.

Vision Care

We work to prevent avoidable blindness and improve quality of life for people who are blind and visually impaired.

Hunger Relief

Our goal is to ensure all community members have access to nutritious food through food drives and meal distribution programs.

Environment

We are committed to restoring our planet's health by protecting and revitalizing the environment through tree planting and clean-up drives.

Childhood Cancer

We support children and families affected by childhood cancer, providing comfort, care, and access to essential treatments.

Community

Strengthening our local community by supporting schools, youth programs, and local initiatives that bring people together.

About The Lions Club of Chennai Marina Lighthouse

As a proud chapter of Lions Club International, we are a group of dedicated volunteers committed to the motto "We Serve." Our club brings together passionate individuals to create positive change in Chennai. Through local projects and global initiatives, we strive to build a kinder, healthier, and more vibrant community for everyone.
